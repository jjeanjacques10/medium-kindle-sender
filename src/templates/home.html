Article Kindle Converter

This is a simple script to convert Medium articles to Kindle format (epub).

Direct sending will be available in the future. For now, you can only download the converted file.

How to use

  1. Copy the URL of the article you want to convert
  2. Paste the URL in the input field above
  3. Click on the "Download epub to Kindle" button
  4. Wait for the conversion to finish
  5. Download the converted file

How to send to Kindle

  1. Send the epub file to your Kindle email address
    (Example: dinosaur@kindle.com)
  2. Verify your identity by email validation
  3. Open your Kindle device and sync the library
